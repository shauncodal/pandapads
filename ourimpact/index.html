Community Upliftment
Providing young women with sanitary towels improves their self-esteem, along with an information booklet that teaches them all about menstruation and what to expect at that time of month. Through our workshops and community engagements, the above is discussed and we stress the importance of having eco-friendly periods. We aim to keep girls in school, empowering them within their communities. This has a knock-on effect where they can participate in activities, positively effecting their future, their families and the ultimately the economy.
Sustainability
Sustainability is so important! If you are going to make a difference, why not make a lasting one. All our products are made with A-grade quality materials, ensuring they last you up to 5 years!
Environmental Impact
Our goal is to reduce global footprint by minimising waste and creating more eco-friendly alternatives for your everyday activities. On average, a woman will use around 600 sanitary pads in 5 years, spending upwards of R1,200.00.
Do you know how long it takes for a single menstrual pad to fully decompose? 500 - 800 YEARS!!!
Gender Equality
We know how tough it is to be a woman, especially in a country with such a high rate of gender-based violence. In South Africa it is estimated that half of the population lives under the poverty line of R992 per month, this means that around 3.7 million girls cannot afford sanitary products, meaning female learners and workers miss an average of 5 days of school and work each month. This results in many young ladies’ never reaching their full potential, ending up in a perpetual cycle of poverty.
